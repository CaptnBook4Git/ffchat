## Rebranding Guide
To rebrand FluffyChat:
1. Update `lib/config/setting_keys.dart` application name.
2. Update `lib/config/app_config.dart` URLs.
3. Add attribution in `lib/utils/platform_infos.dart`.
4. Update English localization in `lib/l10n/intl_en.arb`.
5. Update platform metadata in AndroidManifest, Info.plist, index.html, my_application.cc, and AppInfo.xcconfig.
6. Ensure AGPL headers are present and correctly formatted in all modified files.
7. Replace branding assets in `assets/` and platform-specific folders.
